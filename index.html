About Our Trip

Santander is a picturesque coastal city that shares a striking resemblance with the scenic towns of the Italian Riviera. As our primary residence and surfing activities were based in Somo, we utilized the local ferry service for our daily commute to the city. The program spanned four days, primarily focusing on professional surfing instruction and coastal exploration.
